## English

A tiny bilingual rescue-post and shareable-card generator for stray cats and dogs.

[Open the app](https://estherliu-lab.github.io/pawhelp-writer/) | [GitHub repository](https://github.com/estherliu-lab/pawhelp-writer)

**Scan to open PawHelp**

<img src="assets/pawhelp-qr.png" alt="QR code to open PawHelp" width="150" height="150">

PawHelp Writer helps ordinary people turn a brief encounter with a stray animal into a clear, compassionate rescue post. Choose the animal’s condition, the help needed, the platform, and the tone; PawHelp creates ready-to-share copy and a warm image card without requiring an account.

- Chinese and English interface
- 15 shareable card templates, including 10 new visual styles
- Upload your own animal photo or use a built-in photo
- 1:1, 3:4, 4:5, 9:16, and 16:9 exports
- Mobile-friendly high-resolution image saving and sharing
- Offline-ready PWA
- No login and no server upload

On Android, the app can be used directly inside WeChat. Image saving switches to a full-screen image made for press-and-hold saving. For home-screen access, first check WeChat's top-right menu for **Add to Home screen**; when that option is unavailable, use Favorites/Floating Window or open the page in any phone browser that supports web-app installation.

## 中文

一个帮助普通人快速生成流浪猫狗求助文案和分享卡片的双语小工具。

[打开应用](https://estherliu-lab.github.io/pawhelp-writer/) | [GitHub 仓库](https://github.com/estherliu-lab/pawhelp-writer)

**扫码打开小爪帮帮**

<img src="assets/pawhelp-qr.png" alt="扫码打开小爪帮帮" width="150" height="150">

看到流浪猫狗，却不知道求助信息该怎么写时，只要填写动物情况、所需帮助、发布平台和表达语气，小爪帮帮就会生成可直接转发的文案和温暖图片卡片。无需登录，填写内容不会上传服务器。

- 中英文界面切换
- 15 套可分享图片模板，包含 10 套新增风格
- 可上传自己的动物照片，也可直接使用内置照片
- 支持 1:1、3:4、4:5、9:16、16:9
- 适配手机的高清图片保存与分享
- 支持离线使用和添加到桌面
- 无需注册，内容仅保存在本机

安卓手机可以直接在微信内使用。保存图片时会进入适合长按保存的全屏原图界面。添加桌面时先查看微信右上角菜单是否有“添加到桌面”；如果当前微信版本没有该选项，可先收藏或添加浮窗，也可以使用任何支持网页应用安装的手机浏览器，不限定 Chrome。
